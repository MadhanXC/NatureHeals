# **App Name**: Nature Heals

## Core Features:

- Acupuncture Course Catalog: A comprehensive listing and booking interface for acupuncture educational courses.
- Wellness Practices Directory: Categorized content hubs for Meditation, Mudras, Breathing techniques, and Naturopathy with visual guides.
- Rejuvenation Camp Showcase: A historical portfolio highlighting past rejuvenation camps through imagery and testimonials.
- Guided Meditation Player: An interactive UI element for timed breathing exercises and meditation sessions.
- Healer Inquiry Portal: Contact form for personalized consultation requests and direct healer communication.
- Regulatory and About Content: Dedicated pages for About Us, Contact, and essential legal and policy information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Meadow Green (#2DB360) to provide a bright, energetic, and life-affirming aesthetic.
- Accent color: Vivid Amethyst (#9B59B6) for high-visibility highlights and calls-to-action to evoke spiritual clarity.
- Background color: Clean Mint White (#F9FFF9), keeping the interface fresh, expansive, and calming.
- The font 'Alegreya' (serif) is used throughout for its elegant, intellectual, and rhythmic feel, conveying wisdom.
- Thin-line organic icons depicting botanical elements and anatomical alignment for a clean, natural look.
- Minimalist grid with generous whitespace, using high-resolution nature photography to ground the user experience.
- Soft fades and 'breathing' scale effects on focus elements to maintain the app's meditative vibe.
